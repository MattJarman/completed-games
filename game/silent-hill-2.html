Didn't expect to really like this game when I picked it up, but struggled to put it down after the first hour or so. Incredible atmosphere, great OST, clever puzzles and fantastic story. Only things I didn't like much was some of the voice acting and the boss fights, but honestly that can all be forgiven considering this game came out 20+ years ago.
